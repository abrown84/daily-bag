# Chore Checklist Launch Campaign

## Campaign Brief

**Objective:** Drive app signups and free trial starts
**Target:** Parents/families tired of nagging about chores
**Core Message:** Turn household chores into a game everyone wants to play

### Target Personas

**Primary: "Tired Parent Taylor"**
- Age 30-45, parent of 2+ kids
- Exhausted from constantly reminding family about chores
- Values fairness and teaching responsibility
- Pain: Feels like a nag, resentment builds, chores never done
- Goal: Peaceful home where everyone contributes willingly

**Secondary: "Roommate Manager Riley"**
- Age 22-35, shares living space with roommates
- Frustrated by uneven chore distribution
- Avoids confrontation but wants fairness
- Pain: Passive-aggressive notes, messy common spaces
- Goal: Fair system without awkward conversations

### Key Messages

1. **Stop nagging, start playing** - Gamification makes chores fun
2. **Points mean prizes** - Real rewards (100 pts = $1) drive motivation
3. **See who's pulling their weight** - Leaderboards create healthy competition
4. **Level up your household** - 10 levels with achievements keep engagement high
